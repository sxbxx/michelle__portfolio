Overview

The idea to create the youth awareness mobile app was pitched by Dr Peter Maulder and Russel Best.

I joined Youth Aware as a product designer when it was just a small team. We had three mobile developers and a product manager on board for the project.

They wanted to create two seperate mobile applications to help inform and educate the public about healthy eating and exercise.

I led the product, marketing, UX and UI strategy for the team. I've been extremely fortunate to have part of this journey and have grown tremendously during my time with the team of Youth Aware, some key achievements of which I have listed below:

Turned an idea into a product.

Although I have my own illustration style, it was a new experiance to implement my knowledge of user experiance and design in a mobile game format. It was something that was challenging for me as I have never encountered game development and design.

Juggled many different hats.

Working in an early-stage startup is not easy. Eventhough I was brought onto the team with the idea of bringing their vision into action, there was more to it that just designing it. The sacifice of my own time during the week and weekends creating a brand identity, conducting research, sketching, testing and designing the product alongside the mobile game developers to ensure the assets

Pitched to investors and crowds.

I developed skills in not only designing our pitches but also presenting them to investors and large crowds at events such as Startcon, Slingshot Accelerator programs and Academy Xi (landing 3rd Place in the PitchX competition in 2017).
